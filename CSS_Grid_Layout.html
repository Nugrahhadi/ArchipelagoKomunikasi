PT Archipelago Media Komunikasi

What is an Archipelago?

PT Archipelago Media Komunikasi is a holding company overseeing several media outlets, including Suptoday, Suptoday Originals, and Nusantaranian.

Based in BSD, South Tangerang, Archipelago specializes in agency and media businesses.

Why Archipelago Media Komunikasi?

"Archipelago" derives from the word for island chain, reflecting Indonesia's numerous islands. To connect these islands, communication media are essential, allowing people to access the same information in real-time, regardless of their location.

We hope our presence can spread information as widely as possible, not just in Indonesia but also abroad. Our aim is for Indonesian media to establish a presence overseas and benefit many people.
